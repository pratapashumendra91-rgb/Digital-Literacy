                              Stay Safe Online — Checklist for College Students (India)


                              
 Prevention Tips

 
1.)Use strong, unique passwords for email, banking, and social media accounts.

2.)Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on important apps like email, Instagram, and banking apps.

3.)Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive work like logging into banking or college portals.

4.)Do not click suspicious links in emails, WhatsApp, or SMS (even if they look official).

5.)Limit personal information sharing such as college ID, address, phone number, or timetable.

6.)Check privacy settings on social media so only trusted people can see your posts.

7.)Download apps only from official stores like Google Play Store or Apple App Store.

8.)Verify job or internship offers before sharing documents or paying registration fees.


# UPI / Financial Safety Tips (Important)
1.)Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details — banks never ask for them.

2.)Do not approve unknown "Collect Requests" — approving them sends money, not receives it.

3.)Check QR codes carefully — scanning a QR code can lead to money being deducted.

4.)Verify payment requests from sellers before making transactions.

5.)Report Cybercrime Immediately.

# If you become a victim, report quickly to:

 1.)National Cyber Crime Portal — cybercrime.gov.in
 
 2.)Cyber Crime Helpline 1930 — Call 1930 immediately
