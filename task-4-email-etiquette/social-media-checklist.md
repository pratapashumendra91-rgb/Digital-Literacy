Responsible Social Media Use – Checklist
 Do's
 
Think before posting — Make sure your content is respectful and appropriate
 Protect your privacy — Adjust privacy settings and avoid sharing personal information
Verify information before sharing — Check facts to avoid spreading misinformation
 Maintain professionalism — Remember that employers and professors may view your profile
 Be respectful to others — Engage politely in discussions and avoid arguments
 Use social media for learning and networking — Follow educational pages and professional groups

 
 Don'ts
 
 Don't share personal information — Avoid posting phone numbers, addresses, or passwords
 Don't post offensive or harmful content — Avoid hate speech, bullying, or inappropriate language
 Don't spread rumors or fake news — Always confirm information before sharing
 Don't overshare your location — Avoid posting live locations publicly
 Don't engage in online arguments — Stay calm and avoid unnecessary conflicts
 Don't neglect academic responsibilities — Avoid excessive social media use during study time
