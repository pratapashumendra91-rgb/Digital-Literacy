Responsible Social Media Use – Checklist

 Do's
 
1.)Think before posting — Make sure your content is respectful and appropriate.

2.)Protect your privacy — Adjust privacy settings and avoid sharing personal information.
 
3.)Verify information before sharing — Check facts to avoid spreading misinformation.
 
 4.)Maintain professionalism — Remember that employers and professors may view your profile.

5.) Be respectful to others — Engage politely in discussions and avoid arguments.

6.)Use social media for learning and networking — Follow educational pages and professional groups

 
 Don'ts
 
 1.Don't share personal information — Avoid posting phone numbers, addresses, or passwords.
 
 2.Don't post offensive or harmful content — Avoid hate speech, bullying, or inappropriate language.
 
 3.Don't spread rumors or fake news — Always confirm information before sharing.
 
 4.Don't overshare your location — Avoid posting live locations publicly.
 
 5.Don't engage in online arguments — Stay calm and avoid unnecessary conflicts.
 
 6.Don't neglect academic responsibilities — Avoid excessive social media use during study time
