Social Media Do's and Don'ts:
Do's:
- Be respectful
- Share useful content
- Protect privacy
- Think before posting
- Use strong passwords

Don'ts:
- Don't share personal info
- Don't spread fake news
- Don't use offensive language
- Don't overshare
- Don't click unknown links

Poor communication can create misunderstandings. For example, sending an informal email to a professor may seem disrespectful. This can be avoided by using proper tone and structure.
Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness
Phishing is a common cybercrime where attackers trick users into sharing sensitive information. They send fake emails or messages that look genuine. Students are often targeted due to lack of awareness. This can lead to data theft or financial loss.

Prevention Tips:
- Do not share OTP
- Check website URLs
- Use strong passwords
- Enable 2FA
- Avoid public WiFi for payments
- Verify links before clicking
- Use official apps
- Report to cybercrime.gov.in or call 1930

I was surprised by how common phishing attacks are. I will be more careful while clicking links and sharing information online.
